Global Warming

This is a problem that has been making it during decades, at the beginning it was treated as a big lie or something that only affect the poor ones. Right now is one of the biggest issues that our generation has, our poles are disappearing, the different habitats are in decadence, animals are in process of extortion. The level of the seas are rising so fast, Venice is a clear example of that issue, their houses are starting to being covered. For us the humans, that rising of the temperatures or extortion of some animal species, are having us with hard difficult to continue with our lives.

Possible Solutions:
  • Decrease the polution produced by cars
  • Use more public transport
  • Use more renewable energy
